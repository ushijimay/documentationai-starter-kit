description: Quick steps to start a Skill3 project.
---

## Getting Started

- Login to your Skill3 dashboard
- Click "Create Project"
- Enter a project name
- Click "Create" button

## Add Your First Page

1. Inside your project, click "Add Page"
2. Name your page (e.g., "Introduction")
3. Save and edit content

You now have your first Skill3 documentation project!
