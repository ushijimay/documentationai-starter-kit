---
title: Cannot Log In to Skill3
description: Fix login problems quickly.
---

## Symptoms

- Cannot access Skill3 dashboard
- "Invalid credentials" error

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click "Forgot Password" on login page and follow the email link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check account email">
    Make sure you use the correct email address registered on Skill3.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browser issues">
    Try a private/incognito window or clear browser cache.
  </Step>
</Steps>
