## Create a Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access dashboard">
    Go to `https://dashboard.skill3.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start new project">
    Click the "Create Project" button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill project details">
    Enter your project name and click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first page">
    Select your new project.
    Click "Add Page" and enter a name (e.g., "Welcome").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin editing">
    Click your page and start adding content with the editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>
